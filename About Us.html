About Us

Welcome to Kids Wardrobe, your ultimate destination for children's fashion. We're not just a store; we're a creative journey founded by a student named Trixy De Asis in 2023, during her time in the BSIT 2-3 program. It all began with a vision to create a vibrant and stylish e-commerce platform dedicated to kids' clothing.

Our Story

In 2023, Trixy De Asis, a dedicated student of the BSIT 2-3 program, embarked on an exciting venture. Guided by her passion for both technology and fashion, Trixy decided to take on the challenge presented by her instructor: create a fully functional e-commerce website. Little did she know that this project would evolve into something as delightful as Kids Wardrobe. Trixy's vision was clear: to develop an e-commerce platform that not only provided a seamless shopping experience but also celebrated the beauty of childhood through fashion. With dedication and countless hours of hard work, she brought Kids Wardrobe to life.
