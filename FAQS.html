Frequently Asked Question

What sizes do you carry?

We carry a wide range of sizes for children, starting from newborns up to pre-teen sizes. Our sizes typically range from 0-3 months to 12 years, but availability may vary for specific styles.

How do I determine the right size for my child?

To determine the correct size for your child, we recommend referring to our size chart. It provides specific measurements for each age range and can help you find the best fit. Additionally, we provide detailed product descriptions that may include information about the garment's dimensions.

What materials are your clothes made of?

We prioritize comfort and quality, so our clothes are made from a variety of materials suitable for children's sensitive skin. We use fabrics like cotton, polyester blends, and organic materials to ensure softness, breathability, and durability.

Do you have clothes for special occasions?

Yes, we have a selection of clothes suitable for special occasions such as birthdays, weddings, and holidays. Our collection includes formal wear, party dresses, smart suits, and adorable outfits to make your child look extra special on those memorable days.

Are your clothes safe for my child?

Safety is our top priority. We follow strict quality control measures to ensure our clothes meet safety standards. We use child-friendly dyes and fastenings, and all our products go through rigorous testing to ensure they are free from harmful substances.

Do you offer discounts or promotions?

Yes, we frequently offer discounts, promotions, and seasonal sales. To stay updated on our latest offers, you can sign up for our newsletter or follow us on social media. We also have special loyalty programs for our regular customers.

What is your return/exchange policy?

We understand that sometimes items may need to be returned or exchanged. We have a hassle-free return policy within a specified time frame. Please refer to our return/exchange policy page on our website or contact our customer service for more details.

How can I contact your customer service?

We have a dedicated customer service team ready to assist you. You can reach out to us through our website's contact form or call our customer service helpline during business hours. We're here to help answer any questions or address any concerns you may have!
